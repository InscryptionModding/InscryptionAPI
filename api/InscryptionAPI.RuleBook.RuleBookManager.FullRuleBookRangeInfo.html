    Remarks

    Ability pages have a small square icon on the left side of the page. Stat icon pages have a small square icon on the left side of the rulebook name. Boon pages have a large icon on both sides of the page. Item pages have a large icon on the left side of the page. Unique is unused by the game, so should be avoided here unless you plan on using a unique page template.
